Features

Everything your service business needs

From the first customer call to the final invoice, PlumbPage handles every step so you can focus on the work that matters. Works for plumbing, HVAC, and electrical companies.

Smart Scheduling

Drag-and-drop calendar with technician availability, skill matching, and conflict detection. Schedule jobs in seconds, not minutes.

Live Dispatch Map

See every job and technician on a real-time map. Assign the closest available tech with one click and reduce drive time.

Invoicing & Estimates

Generate professional invoices and multi-option estimates from the field. Accept payments online via credit card or financing.

Customer Management

Full customer profiles with contact info, job history, equipment records, and a self-service portal for booking requests.

Technician App

Your techs get a mobile-friendly app to view jobs, clock in/out, add notes, capture signatures, and update job status on site.

Inventory & Pricebook

Track parts and materials with low-stock alerts. Maintain a standardized pricebook for consistent quoting across your whole team.
